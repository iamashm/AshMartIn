About

I'm ashmartin — a designer and builder based somewhere quiet. I make tools for thinking and reading, and I write about the process: the constraints, the tradeoffs, and the things that didn't work.

Most of my work lives at the edge of design and engineering. I care about things that are useful without being complicated, and beautiful without being decorative.
